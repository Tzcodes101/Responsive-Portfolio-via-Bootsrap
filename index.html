About Me

I just got a dog who is an Aussiedoodle named Arwin. Aussiedoodles are an Australian Shepherd and Poodle mix. Arwin is insanely smart. This page is dedicated to looking at how cute she is. If you don't love Aussiedoodles, I hope you love dogs, and if not dogs, then cats. Hopefully you love some animal, but Aussiedoodles are especially cute!

Arwin loves all food, but she is still learning to play with toys. She is a puppy, born June 1st 2020, so she likes to bite my legs as I walk. Still, she already knows sit and how to wait for treats. When she comes inside, she goes in to her crate because she knows she gets a piece of freeze-dried liver whenever she comes inside (gross but she loves it). Arwin is very charming and lovely, except during Puppy Witching Hour. Then, she is crazy and there is no talking sense in to her. I hope you think she's cute!
